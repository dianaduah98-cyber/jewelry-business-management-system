# Project Charter

## Project Name

Jewelry Business Management System

---

## Purpose

Develop the project plan for a centralized platform that supports the daily operations of a jewelry business.

---

## Objectives

- Improve inventory management
- Organize customer information
- Simplify supplier management
- Reduce manual work
- Increase operational efficiency

---

## Stakeholders

- Founder
- Customers
- Suppliers
- Future Development Team

---

## Success Criteria

- Clear project documentation
- Defined business requirements
- Complete project roadmap
- Test plan prepared
